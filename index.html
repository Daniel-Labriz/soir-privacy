SOIR — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

SOIR helps a group find an evening that works for everyone. A host opens a session and shares a short join code; each person's device reads their calendars to work out which candidate evenings they are free, and the app reports only that free/busy result so the group can pick a date.

The short version

SOIR does not collect personal information. There are no accounts, no passwords, no email addresses, no advertising, and no third-party analytics or tracking. We never read or transmit the contents of your calendar events.

What the app accesses

  • Calendar (on your device only). With your permission, SOIR reads your calendars to determine whether you are busy on the candidate evenings. This happens entirely on your device. The only thing that leaves your device is a simple free/busy verdict (true/false) for each proposed date. Event titles, attendees, locations, notes, times, and all other event details never leave your device.

What is sent to and stored on our server

To coordinate the group, the following is stored for the duration of a session:

  • A display name you type when creating or joining a session (you may use any name or nickname).
  • An anonymous participant identifier (a random value) generated for you — it is not tied to your identity, email, or device.
  • The session details: its title (optional), the candidate dates, and each participant's free/busy verdict per date.

Identity in SOIR is anonymous — the join code is the only secret. We do not know who you are.

Data retention

Sessions and all their data are automatically deleted approximately 30 days after the session is created. We do not keep the data beyond that.

Where data is stored

Session data is stored on servers hosted in the European Union (Sweden). It is used solely to run the scheduling feature and is never sold or shared with third parties.

Children

SOIR is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect information from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
